Questions?

Is it only for dogs? +

Not at all. Anything the household looks after can be a character - the cat, the houseplants, the fish, even the wheelie bin.

Do I need NFC tags? +

No - ticking chores off in the app works just fine. Tags are an optional shortcut: stick one where the chore happens and tap your phone on it as you go.
