Have You Fed The Dog?

The cosy family chore tracker. The dog, the cat, the plants — even the wheelie bin — become friendly characters. Tick their chores off with a tap, and everyone in the house knows it's done.

Friendly characters

Everything the household looks after becomes a character with moods — happy when fed, sad when forgotten, asleep at night.

📳

Tap an NFC tag

Stick a tag by the dog food. Tap your phone on it as you scoop — chore logged, no app-opening required.

🏆

Streaks & awards

Everyone gets a push when a chore's done. Streaks, a weekly leaderboard, and tongue-in-cheek awards keep it fun.
